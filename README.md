# Data Analytics Portfolio

### **This repository showcases my end-to-end data analytics projects focused on SQL, business insights, segmentation, and engagement strategy.**

# Projects

## 1. SQL – Social Media Engagement Analysis

Analyzed relational Instagram data (7 tables) to evaluate engagement patterns, user segmentation, influencer efficiency, and content performance using SQL.

🔹Identified 26% inactive users as a major retention opportunity

🔹 Discovered engagement concentration among top 15–20% creators

🔹 Measured strong correlation between posting frequency and engagement growth

🔹 Determined engagement per post as a stronger influencer metric than follower count

🔹 Analyzed hashtag performance showing dominance of emotion & lifestyle content

Developed a SQL-based analytical framework to support segmentation-driven marketing strategies and platform growth decisions.

## 2️. Excel – AstroSage Call Center Performance Analysis

🔹Analyzed 28,027 consultation records to evaluate:

🔹Call completion efficiency (40.55% completion rate identified)

🔹Revenue dependency on call services (78.7% contribution)

🔹Agent workload imbalance

🔹Customer satisfaction gaps (Avg rating: 2.93)

## 3. Power BI – Bank CRM Customer Churn Analysis

Developed an end-to-end churn analysis solution using Excel, SQL, and Power BI to identify key drivers of customer churn.

🔹Identified higher churn among inactive customers (26.85%) vs active (14.27%)

🔹Found significant churn in Germany (32.44%) compared to other regions

🔹Observed higher churn for customers with 1 product (27.71%) vs 2 products (7.58%)

🔹Highlighted 50+ age group (44.65%) as high-risk segment

🔹Built interactive dashboards for churn analysis and business insights
